Goat Cheese Naan Flatbread with Blackberries

Description:

This recipe is a great way to use more blackberries, which are starting to reach their peak season in the summer! This recipe uses naan bread as a flatbread, and it perfect for one person.

Melting the blackberries, goat cheese, and balsamic vinaigrette produce a melt in your mouth bite that pairs perfectly with some prosciutto and arugula. Eat it alone as a sweet and savory lunch, or make multiple and cut them up for a charcuterie board. The possibilities are endless!

Ingredients:

  • Naan
  • Blackberries
  • Goat cheese
  • Balsamic Glaze
  • Arugula
  • Honey
  • Prosciutto

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Place Naan Flatbread on the oven rack and bake for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from the oven and add balsamic glaze and goat cheese. Add fresh blackberries.
  4. Place in the oven on the rack for 5 minutes. Remove and add on a baking sheet for an additional 2 minutes in the oven.
  5. Using a fork, gently mash the blackberries and goat cheese.
  6. Remove from the oven and top iwth arugula, prosciutto, and a drizzle of honey.
